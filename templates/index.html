What everyone should know:

Compost

Don't put in:

  • wax and wax coated cups and cartons
  • oils and grease
  • glossy magazine covers and wrapping paper
  • dirty cardboard and paper cups
  • pet waste

Do put in:

  • fruit and veggie scraps
  • stale and moldy breads and grains
  • old herbs and spices
  • coffee grounds and paper filters
  • tea and tea bags
  • wool and cotton

Recycle

Do not put in:

  • greasy cardboard
  • plastic grocery bags
  • styrofoam
  • plastic bottle caps
  • shredded or brightly colored paper
  • batteries

Do put in:

  • metal
  • glass
  • clean paper and cardboard
  • aluminum food cans
  • corks

Landfill

Do not put in:

  • car batteries
  • motor oil
  • tires
  • liquid waste
  • cathode ray tube (CRT) screens
  • products containing mercury

Do put in:

  • plastic straws
  • wrappers
  • plastic bags
  • confetti
  • clothes
